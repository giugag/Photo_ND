Photo_ND

Neurodiversity through Photography in Research and Education

About the Project

Photo_ND: Neurodiversity through Photography in Research and Education is a research project exploring neurodivergent experiences through creative and inclusive methodologies.

The project consists of two complementary initiatives. The first is a photovoice initiative, inviting neurodivergent individuals to share their lived experiences of sleep difficulties and light sensitivity using photography and narrative as a form of expression. The second focuses on inclusive teaching in creative arts, examining barriers and knowledge gaps encountered by neurodivergent students and educators, and supporting greater awareness of neurodiverse needs and strengths within higher education.

Photo_ND is supported by a DkIT–Maynooth University Research Collaboration Seed Fund and emphasises public engagement, accessibility, and inclusive research practice.
