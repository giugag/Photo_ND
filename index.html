About the Project

Photo_ND: Neurodiversity through Photography in Research and Education is a research project exploring neurodivergent experiences through inclusive and creative methodologies.

The project brings together two complementary strands: a photovoice initiative, inviting neurodivergent individuals to express lived experiences of sleep difficulties and light sensitivity through photography and narrative; and a focus on inclusive teaching in creative arts, examining barriers and knowledge gaps experienced by neurodivergent students and educators.

Photo_ND is supported by a DkIT–Maynooth University Research Collaboration Seed Fund and places accessibility, public engagement, and inclusive research practice at its core.
